# Logo

The `Logo` is used to display the Eu/Acc community movement logo. There are four different logo variants, which allows our community or media to cover a wide number of different uses. These variations are [Long](#long-variation), [Short](#short-variation), [Micro](#micro-variation), [Pictorial](#pictorial-variation), and [3D](#3d-variation).

## Long Variation

This is the full version of our logo. It's ideal for headers, banners, or any place where you want the complete Eu/Acc community movement name to stand out.
