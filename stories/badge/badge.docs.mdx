# Badge

The `Badge` is used on digital websites or media to show support for the Eu/Acc community movement. There are two different support badge variants, which allows our community or media to use on a wide variation of different backgrounds. These variations are [Long Horizontal](#long-horizontal-variation), [Long Vertical](#long-vertical-variation), [Short Horizontal](#short-horizontal-variation), and [Short Vertical](#short-vertical-variation).
